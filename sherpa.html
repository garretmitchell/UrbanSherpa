MOST RELIABLE NYC SKI & SNOWBOARD TRIPS

With thirty years of reliable NYC ski and snowboard bus trips to Hunter and Windham Mountains, Urban Sherpa dominates today’s winter adventure industry and remains New York City’s most popular skiing and snowboarding bus operator. New Yorkers ride several days a week to area mountains, with varied options for purchasing lift tickets, rentals, and lessons. Join our experienced leaders aboard our Urban Sherpa luxury motor coaches for single day adventures.

Urban Sherpa quickly established itself as the preeminent day trip adventure operator out of New York City. We specialize in single-day trips, a necessity for our urban professional, whose time is measured, by helping to make the introductory enthusiast more confident or assisting the expert prepare for his or her next big adventure. Your Urban Sherpa knows when, where, and how to get you there. Urban Sherpa will enable you to enliven your otherwise indoor lifestyle by reacquainting you with the outdoors, and we aren’t just going to run down to the local park. It’s simple: Urban Sherpa has helped thrill-seekers do just that, and we do it with an expected New York City flair—no hype, no guff.
